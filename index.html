Hi, I'm Bronson Roybal

Web Application & Front End Developer

When I was just a kid, the sweet cacophony of the 56k modem connecting was music to my ears, as I knew it was my ticket to unbridled web surfing freedom. One day, however, my patience had run dry and the siren call had revealed its true nature as a restrictive racket. I had just discovered Call of Duty, multiplayer. I was hooked and dial-up was no longer my savoir. Trying to play multiplayer video games on a 56k connection should have been one of Hercules’ labors. It’s a torturous act; an evil I wish on no other human.

Long story short, I needed to learn about computer hardware and internet speeds as fast as possible. This spawned my love of all things technology. Computers had suddenly, out of nowhere, become my lifelong passion. Coding was always a dream that seemed out of grasp, but now I’m living it and loving every second.
